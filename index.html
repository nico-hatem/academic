[Website in progress] Welcome! I am a Postdoctoral fellow at CentraleSupelec working on issues in environmental and energy economics.

My research is on climate change mitigation policies, especially for reducing the carbon intensity of energy. I am interested in exploring the efficiency conditions of the deployment of low-carbon energy technologies, such as solar photovoltaic or green hydrogen, given different levels of governance and of spatial scattering.

I have recently acquired a PhD from the Paris School of Economics (December 2024).
